About Us

Little Ones Preschool

Little Ones is a preschool that caters to early childhood education by providing them a safe environment and the right methods with plenty of opportunities to play, learn, explore and grow. We believe Learning should be fun and adventure.


SEE PHOTO GALLERY

Curriculum

Our Curriculum is designed to focus on overall development of a child based on play-way method, where all activities are carefully structured based on their themes, keeping in mind the unique personality of every child helping in different stages of the child's mental and physical growth. It introduces learning to the child as fun.

The areas we focus are :

  • Cognitive Development
  • Physical Development
  • Language Development
  • Numerical Development
  • Social and Emotional Development
  • Self Help / Adaptive Development

Age Group

Our preschool has four classes for the kids aged from 1.5 yrs to 6 yrs.

  • Play Group - 1.5 yrs to 3 yrs
  • Nursery - 2.5 yrs to 4 yrs
  • Junior KG - 3.5 yrs to 5 yrs.
  • Senior KG - 4.5 yrs to 6 yrs.​
